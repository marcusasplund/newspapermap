privacy policy

Great Name (through all our sites and apps as listed here and where underlying pages) protects the privacy of all visitors who use the services we provide on our website. We have a simple policy of not to give out personal information about our users without first getting their permission, unless required by law.

Cookies

First, we don't use cookies at all.
A cookie is a small text file sent to your browser from a web server and stored by your browser. It contains data that websites use to identify unique visitors. Companies that advertise on our site and locations (eg, newspapers) that you may reach from the site may collect information from you if you click on their ads or visit their Web site. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. You can change the settings of your browser to refuse all cookies or notify you when a cookie is sent to your computer, giving you the choice whether to accept it or not. If you have any questions or concerns about the manner in which information is collected or used by another site, you should contact the person responsible for the site directly.

Your Privacy

Great Name does not collect any personal information about you (such as name or email address) unless you specifically and knowingly provide such information. Our analytical toolshttp://www.google.com/analytics/ analyzes IP addresses from users who visits our site but don´t collect or reveal those. This information is used by us for internal administration, and analyze how visitors use our site, so that we can continue to improve the services we offer. The information also provides us with anonymous and broad demographic information about users of our site.

Third Parties

We may share information about the use of our apps (as listed here and where underlying pages) with advertisers, sponsors, content providers and other third parties. But we share only aggregated and anonymous information about our users and will not give out any personal information to third parties without your explicit consent. For example, we may disclose the total number of “hits” the site may be, or how often the average user visits our site. However, we can share specific personal information we hold about you if required by law.

Email

We receive and respond to email from users on a regular basis. For administrative purposes we retain copies of all incoming and outgoing e-mail. Information in the email we receive or send will not be disclosed to any third party without permission from the sender, either express or implied, unless required by law.

Our apps (as listed here and where underlying pages) contain numerous links to external sites.
